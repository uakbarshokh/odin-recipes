Baked Lemon-Herb Salmon with Quinoa & Asparagus

Description

This is a clean, protein-packed meal that comes together in under 30 minutes. The bright acidity of the lemon perfectly cuts through the richness of the salmon.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch fresh asparagus (woody ends snapped off)
  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 lemon (sliced) plus 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill or parsley (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Quinoa: In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water/broth. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, cover, and cook for 15 minutes until the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.
  2. Prep the oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Arrange and season: Place the salmon fillets on one side of the pan and the asparagus on the other. Drizzle 2 tbsp of olive oil over everything.
  4. Flavor the salmon: Rub the minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and dill over the salmon. Top each fillet with a slice or two of fresh lemon. Toss the asparagus in the remaining oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bake: Roast in the oven for 12-15 minutes, depending on the thickness of the salmon, until the fish flakes easily with a fork and the asparagus is tender.
  6. Serve: Plate the salmon and asparagus over a bed of the fluffy quinoa.
